Frequently-Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Lectio?

Lectio is a website that allows you to track your reading progress on behalf of your children!

How does it work?

Put in information about new books your children have started reading, log the number of pages they have read, and that's it! You can see your child's reading history as the days go by!

Will the site be updated to include new features?

Definitely! We are constantly adding new features and updates to make Lectio the number 1 reading tracker for people of all ages!
